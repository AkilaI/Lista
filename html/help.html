User can view companies listed in following categories by click in respective thumbnails in the Home Page.

  • Bank
  • Hotel
  • Hospital
  • Fashion
  • Super Market
  • Hardware
  • Tele Communtication
  • Other

User can create account clicking Signup Button in the navigation bar

Users with user account can add following details of their company to the system.

  • Company Name
  • Location
  • Category
  • Experience
  • Contact Number

Important

Admin can remove Users or Company Details added by users upon a valid complain.
